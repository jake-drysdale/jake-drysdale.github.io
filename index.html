About Me


Hello, I’m Jake!

I work on audio and machine learning research with the aim to develop systems for enhancing the music production process and discovering new sounds.

My PhD thesis, titled “Deep Learning for Sample-based Music Production”, proposes deep learning-based systems for analysing electronic music and generating samples.

Outside of research I create and perform music under the alias Drytek.
